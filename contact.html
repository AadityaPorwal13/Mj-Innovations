Common Questions

Before you reach out,
here's what people ask.

Is MJ Innovations already deployed with real clients?

We're a new company — and we're completely upfront about that. We don't yet have a portfolio of live enterprise clients. What we do have is deep technical expertise, a production-ready platform, and a genuine commitment to building trust through results rather than promises. We offer free 30-day pilots with no long-term commitment so you can evaluate performance in your own environment before making any decision.

What does a pilot programme look like?

We connect to a subset of your existing cameras — typically 5 to 20 — configure detection zones and alert rules together with you, then run live for 30 days at zero cost. You get full dashboard access, real-time alerts, and a detailed performance report at the end. No hardware investment, no contract, no obligation to continue. If you're not impressed, we part ways on good terms — and we'll tell you honestly what worked and what didn't.

Do we need to replace our existing cameras?

No — and this is a core part of our value proposition. MJ Innovations connects to any RTSP or ONVIF-compatible camera, which covers the vast majority of CCTV and IP cameras on the market. For older analog systems, Device X can handle the conversion. You keep your existing infrastructure and invest nothing in new hardware — we add the AI intelligence layer on top of what you already have.

How do you handle data privacy and compliance?

Your footage never leaves your environment unless you explicitly choose our managed cloud option. On-premise and Device X deployments are fully local — no external data transmission. All stored data is AES-256 encrypted. We sign data processing agreements with all clients and are built for GDPR, HIPAA, and ISO 27001 compliance from day one. Forensic search and analytics are processed locally; only alert metadata is synced to your dashboard.

What's your pricing structure?

We use a camera-based subscription model — you pay per active camera per month. Volume discounts apply from 50+ cameras, with significant reductions at 200+ and 1,000+. Enterprise on-premise licensing is quoted based on your specific deployment. We don't publish fixed pricing publicly because every environment is different — but we give completely transparent proposals within 24 hours of an enquiry, with no hidden fees and no lock-in clauses.

How quickly can we go from enquiry to live AI?

For most deployments: same week. A typical pipeline is — enquiry on Monday, demo on Tuesday, pilot configured Wednesday, live by Thursday. If you have ONVIF or RTSP cameras and our platform, setup is under 30 minutes. For larger enterprise deployments with complex integrations, we build a realistic timeline together — but we never artificially extend it.
