Common Questions

Before you reach out,
here's what people ask.

Is MJ Innovations already deployed with real clients?

We're a new company — and we're upfront about that. We don't yet have a portfolio of enterprise clients. What we do have is deep technical expertise, a production-ready platform, and a genuine commitment to building trust through results. We offer pilot deployments with no long-term commitment, so you can see the AI perform in your environment before making any decision.

What does a pilot deployment look like?

We connect to a subset of your existing cameras — typically 5 to 20 — configure detection zones and alert rules with you, and run live for 30 days at no cost. You get full access to the dashboard, real alerts, and a performance report at the end. No hardware investment, no contract, no obligation to continue.

Do we need to replace our existing cameras?

No. Our platform connects to any RTSP or ONVIF-compatible camera — which covers the vast majority of CCTV and IP cameras on the market. If you have an older analog system, Device X can handle that too. You keep your existing infrastructure; we add the AI layer on top.

How do you handle data privacy?

Your footage never leaves your environment unless you choose our managed API option. On-premise and Device X deployments are fully air-gapped — no data is ever transmitted externally. All stored footage is AES-256 encrypted. We sign data processing agreements (DPAs) with all clients and are designed for GDPR compliance from the ground up.

How long does deployment actually take?

A typical deployment — connecting cameras, configuring zones, setting alert rules — takes between 20 and 45 minutes depending on the number of cameras. We provide a step-by-step onboarding guide, and our team is available via video call to walk through setup in real time if needed.

What's the pricing model?

We use a camera-based subscription model — you pay per camera per month, with volume discounts from 50+ cameras. Enterprise pricing for on-premise deployments is quoted based on your specific setup. We don't publish pricing publicly because every deployment is different — reach out and we'll give you a clear, transparent quote within 24 hours.
