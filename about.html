Innovation Focus

Core R&D Pillars.

As we build the MJ-Vision platform, our engineering teams are relentlessly focused on solving the three hardest problems in computer vision and proactive security.

Zero-Latency Edge Inference

Developing proprietary hardware architectures that process multi-class object detection directly on the camera node, eliminating round-trip cloud delays and bandwidth costs entirely.

Contextual Behaviour Graphs

Moving beyond simple bounding boxes. Our research focuses on temporal relationships between entities—understanding intent and predicting incidents before they physically occur.

Adversarial Robustness

Hardening our neural networks against sophisticated attacks, lighting anomalies, and environmental obfuscation to ensure military-grade reliability in unpredictable real-world scenarios.
