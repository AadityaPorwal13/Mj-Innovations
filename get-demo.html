Common Questions

Questions about the demo.

Q
Do I need to connect my own cameras for the demo?
Not at all. We run the demo on our own live feed environment so you can see everything working without needing to connect anything. If you'd prefer to use your own camera, we can arrange that too — just mention it in the form.
Q
Who will be on the call with me?
An MJ Innovations AI security engineer — not a salesperson. They built the platform and can answer any technical question in depth. You may also have a second person join for specific domain expertise (e.g. healthcare compliance, government security).
Q
Is there any commitment after the demo?
Absolutely none. The demo is completely free with zero obligation. We believe the platform speaks for itself — we'd rather show you than pressure you. You'll receive a written proposal, and the decision is entirely yours.
Q
We're a new company exploring options — is this demo suitable?
Yes, and we especially welcome early conversations. MJ Innovations is also a new company building something we believe in. We're happy to do early-stage demos, discuss requirements, and grow with organisations from the ground up.
Q
We only have a few cameras — is MJ Innovations still relevant?
Absolutely. Device X works from a single camera upward. Many of our best use cases start small — one building, one perimeter, one critical zone — and expand over time. There's no minimum camera requirement.
Q
What happens to the data I provide in this form?
Your information is used only to schedule and prepare your demo. We don't sell it, share it, or add you to unsolicited marketing lists. You can request deletion at any time — see our Privacy Policy for full details.
